Deviled Eggs

Description

Deviled eggs are a delicious take on the classic hard-boiled egg. The main difference is the consistency of the yolk. In deviled eggs, the yolk is mixed with mayonaise and mustard to make it creamier. This recipe will yield twelve deviled egg halves.

See recipe below for ingredients and directions.

Ingredients

  • 6 hard-boiled eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of mayonaise
  • 1 teaspoon of white sugar, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon of prepared mustard
  • 1 tablespoon of finely chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon of finely chopped celery
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt, or to taste
  • 1 pinch of paprika, or to tast

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Peel hard-boiled eggs.
  2. Slice eggs in half lengthwise and remove yolks. Set whites aside.
  3. Mash yolks with fork in a small bowl. Stir in mayonaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard, onion, and celery. Mix well and season with salt to taste.
  4. Stuff or pipe egg yolk mixture into egg whites.
  5. Sprinkle with paprika. Refrigerate until serving.
